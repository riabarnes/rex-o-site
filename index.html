About REX-O

REX-O develops AI-powered unmanned surface vehicles (USVs) designed for both maritime defence and agritech applications. From precision strike and swarm surveillance missions to automated feeder systems for fish and prawn farming, REX-O brings autonomy, efficiency, and scalability to the water.

About REX-O

REX-O Defence Systems is pioneering the future of naval warfare with our AI-powered, expendable kamikaze Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs). Designed for stealth, autonomy, and scalability, REX-O platforms combine real-time surveillance, precision strike capability, and swarm coordination to excel in modern asymmetric warfare and coastal defense scenarios.

Key Features

  • Stealth-mode deployment in hostile zones
  • Real-time autonomous navigation
  • AI-driven mission-specific targeting
  • Swarm coordination for distributed impact
  • Low-cost, high-efficiency platform

Applications

REX-O systems are ideal for:

  • Coastal surveillance & maritime border control
  • Risk-free patrol in conflict zones
  • Special naval operations requiring autonomy
  • High-speed strategic strike missions
